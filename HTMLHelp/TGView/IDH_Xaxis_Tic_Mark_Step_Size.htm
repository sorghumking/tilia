X-axis Tic Mark Step Size

 

If the Automatic is checked in the Step Size panel in the Tic Marks tab sheet in the X-axes Dialog Box, tic marks will be assigned automatically. If the Automatic is not checked, enter the step sizes for the minor, major, and labeled tic marks. Labeled tic marks should be the same as or a multiple of the major tic marks.

 

In the example below:

 

minor tic marks = 5

major tic marks = 10

labeled tic marks = 20.
