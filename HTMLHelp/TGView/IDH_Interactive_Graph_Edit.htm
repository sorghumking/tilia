Interactively Editing Graphs

To select a graph, place the cursor over the desired graph and click the left mouse button until the graph is highlighted. Because graphs overlap, more than one mouse click may be necessary. When a graph is highlighted, the Graph toolbar will appear:

 

 

X-axis Dialog Box

Graph Style Dialog Box

Skips highlighted graph

Moves graph one position left

Moves graph one position right

 

The highlighted graph can be moved several positions at once by simultaneously pressing , placing the cursor over the highlighted graph, pressing the left mouse button, and moving the graph to the desired location while keeping the mouse button and depressed.

 

Right clicking the highlighted graph pops up the Graph Style Dialog Box.

 

Clicking on the main toolbar will remove the selection rectangle and the Graph toolbar.
