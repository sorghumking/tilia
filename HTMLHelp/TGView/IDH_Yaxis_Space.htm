Space Between Scale and First Graph

 

 

 

You can specify the space in virtual units between the Y-axis scale and the Y-axis of the first graph. The default value is 0.2.

 

Tip:

  • If the space between the scale and the first graph is zero, the scale and Y-axis are merged so that the tic marks appear on the Y-axis of the first graph.
