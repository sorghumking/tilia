Zone Levels

Enter zone levels (depths) in the Levels tab sheet in the Zones Dialog Box.

 

 

 

 

Insert or delete levels with the following buttons:

 Insert new level.

 Delete level.

Tips

  • To enter the first zone level in an empty worksheet, press .

  • To sort the zones by level, click the Level column header. A second click reverses the sort order (ascending order is changed to descending and vice versa). The small arrow displayed in the column header indicates the current sort order. The up-arrow indicates the ascending sort order, the down-arrow – descending.
