Font Formatting

The following HTML-like tags are used for italics, bold, superscripts, subscripts, and Greek letters. They are used in pairs to enclose the text to be formatted.

For Greek letters, use uppercase names (e.g. SIGMA) for uppercase letters and lowercase names (e.g. sigma) for lowercase letters. For a table of Greek letter names, click here.

Because the less than (<) and greater than (>) characters are used for tags, HTML/XML "entity references" are used for these characters. An ampersand (&) initiates an entity reference, and a semicolon (;) terminates it. Because the ampersand (&) initiates an entity reference, it also requires an entity reference of its own.
