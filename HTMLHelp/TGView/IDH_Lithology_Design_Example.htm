Lithology Sample

Sample of designed pattern. On the diagram, the lithology column is 1.2 virtual units wide. This sample is 120 pixels wide. For design purposes, the Troels-Smith vector patterns are optimized for the display as bitmaps. They will look even better on a high-resolution printer.

 

The pattern shown is for the lithologic unit highlighted in the worksheet. Only one bitmap pattern can be selected, but up to four Troels-Smith patterns can be overlaid. The Troels-Smith patterns can be shifted by clicking the buttons on the shift pad:

 

 

The pattern shifted will be the one highlighted in the lithologic component tab panel below.
