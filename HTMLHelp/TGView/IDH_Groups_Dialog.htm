Groups Dialog Box

The Groups Dialog Box is accessed by clicking Groups under the Options menu or by clicking on the toolbar. The first graph, last graph, and name are designated for each group.

 

 

 

To enter the first and last graph of each group, click on the right side of the column to display a list box for selection of the graph:
