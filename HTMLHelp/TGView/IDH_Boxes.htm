Boxes

To draw boxes around graph names, click Boxes on the Options menu. A check mark to the left of Boxes indicates that Boxes are selected. Click again to de-select.

 

 

For boxes to be drawn, graph names must be drawn at a 90° angle. Set the angle on the Name tab sheet of the X-axes Dialog Box. Below is an example.

 

 

Tip

  • Boxes work best if the names are centered over the graphs. Center the names on the Name tab sheet of the X-axes Dialog Box.
