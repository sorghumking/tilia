The Y-axis height is designated in inches or cm. This height is the distance between the Minimum and Maximum Axis Limits, which are designated in the Y-axes Dialog Box. The diagram will be printed over multiple pages if necessary. This option is particularly useful for printing different diagrams with the same vertical scaling. For example, if the vertical span of one diagram is 6,000 years and another is 10,000 years, printing the first diagram with a Y-axis height of 6.0 and the second diagram with a Y-axis height of 10.0 will produce diagrams with the same vertical scaling of 1 cm/1000 yr.
