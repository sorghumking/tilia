Interactive Editing

The diagram may be edited interactively by placing the cursor over the desired object and clicking the left mouse button. The selected object will by highlighted by a dotted line surrounding it. If objects lie on top of each other, click the mouse button until the desired object is highlighted. Once the desired object is highlighted, click the right mouse button to pop up a dialog box for editing options.

 

The following objects may be edited interactively:

 

Graphs

Y-axes

Zones

Zone labels

Groups

Dates

Text
