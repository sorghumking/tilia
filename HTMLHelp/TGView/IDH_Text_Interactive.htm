Interactive Text

 

 

To enter text interactively, click , which will cause the cursor to change to a crosshair. Then click the crosshair at the point of text insertion and enter text in the New Text Dialog Box. The point of insertion will be the lower left corner of the text string.

 

Text can be formatted with the font formatting buttons.

 

Click to change the font.

 

 

Tip

  • The X and Y coordinates of the crosshair cursor appear in the status bar at the bottom of the window. Use the Interactive Text button to determine the virtual coordinates of any point on the diagram. Clicking the Interactive Text button a second time will cause the normal cursor to reappear.
