Lithologic Unit Worksheet

Enter the top and bottom depths of lithologic units. When beginning a new lithology column, press to enter the first unit. New units can be entered at the bottom of the worksheet, or click to insert a new row. Click to delete a row. Click the checkbox in the Lower Bound column to draw a line across the lithology column at the bottom of the lithologic unit. Click in the Top Depth column to expand the record and show the lithologic units that have been selected. See example below.

 

 

The expanded record cannot be edited directly; rather changes must be made by selections in the dialog box.
