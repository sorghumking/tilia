Changing the Order of Variables on the Diagram

Clicking or in the Variables Dialog Box will move the selected variable down or up. A group of variables may be selected and moved up or down with the arrow buttons. Select groups with one of the following methods:

  1. Press while repeatedly pressing or .

  2. Press or while clicking rows with the mouse. Pressing will select all rows between the current row and the last selected. Pressing will select only the rows clicked.

To move variables more than one row at a time, highlight the variables to be moved then click . Move to the insertion point and click or to insert above or below the current row.

 

Graphs may also be moved interactively.
