X-axis Tic Mark Lengths

 

The lengths of tic marks on the X-axis are entered in the Tic Mark Lengths panel on the Tic Marks tab sheet in the X-axes Dialog Box. For the bottom axis, the major and minor tic marks can have different lengths. The tic marks for the top axis are all the same length. Tic mark lengths are in virtual units.
