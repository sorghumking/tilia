Truncate X-axis

 

 

Click the Truncate check box on the Truncate tab sheet on the X-axes Dialog Box to truncate the graph at the end of the axis or at the beginning of the next graph, based on the Maximum Axis Limit on the Axis tab sheet in the X-axes Dialog Box.

 

 

Options exist for:

 

Where to truncate

Printing the truncated values
