Units of Measurement

TGView utilizes a variety of units of measurement:

  1. Natural units are the units in which the variable is measured (e.g. percentage, cm, grains/cm3).

  2. The diagram is constructed in arbitrary Virtual units, which may be thought of as the units on a large piece of graph paper on which diagram is drawn. The diagram is then projected to fit a display or printed page in actual units. For more information click here.

  3. Actual units are units in which the diagram is actually printed. Line widths are set in actual point sizes.
