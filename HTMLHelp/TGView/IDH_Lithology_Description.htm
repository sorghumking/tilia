Lithology

A Lithology column can be placed on the diagram between the Y-axis scale and the first graph. Options for lithology are designated in the Lithology Dialog Box.  Two kinds of patterns can be selected: bitmap patterns and Troels-Smith vector patterns. The properties of each are listed below:

 

Bitmap patterns:

  • Are optimized for the device and are device dependent.

  • Their appearance on a display and printer may differ.

  • They will usually look good on a display.

  • They cannot be overlaid.

Troels-Smith patterns:

  • Are device independent.

  • Appearance on different devices is the same except as limited by the resolution of the device.

  • May not look good on a display, but will look good on a high-resolution printer (300 dpi or greater).

  • Can be overlaid to show different sediment components.

A lithology column can have both bitmap and Troels-Smith patterns for different lithologic units, but only one style can be used for any individual unit.
