Text Dialog Box

The Text Dialog Box is accessed by clicking Text under the Options menu or by clicking on the toolbar. For examples of different categories of text, click here.

 

 

 

The Diagram Limits are shown at the bottom of the dialog box. The limits are the maximum extent of the diagram in virtual units. They are useful for positioning text on the diagram.
