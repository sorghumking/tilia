Position of Symbol on X-Axis of Presence Graph

The X-axis position (or distance from the vertical axis) of the symbols on a Presence graph is entered in the edit box for Position of Symbol on X-axis on the Presence Graph tab sheet in the Graph Style Dialog Box. The position is measured in the normal X-axis coordinates. For a percentage pollen diagram, the units would be percent.

 

The minor tic mark will be set equal to the position of the symbol on the X-axis. The major tic mark will be set equal to two times the minor tic mark. The length of the X-axis will be equal to the major tic mark. These options can be further changed on the Tic Marks tab sheet in the X-axes Dialog Box.
