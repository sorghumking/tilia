Lithology Shift Pad

Click one of the arrow buttons to shift a Troels-Smith pattern up, down, right, or left. Click the center button to return the pattern to its original position. The pattern shifted will be the one highlighted in the lithologic component tab panel below.

 

Overlaid patterns sometimes interact to produce an undesirable visual effect. Shifting a pattern can improve the overall appearance. Usually only overlaid patterns require shifting. Bitmap patterns cannot be shifted.
